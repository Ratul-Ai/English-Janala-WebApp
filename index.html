Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website? +

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use? +

Yes, this website is completely free to use. You can access most content without any payment.

3. Do I need to create an account? +

No, creating an account is optional. Signing up lets you track progress and save your work.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary? +

Build your English vocabulary by reading regularly and practicing daily. Using flashcards, apps, or writing exercises can speed up learning.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses? +

Some courses offer certificates upon completion. These certificates can be downloaded or shared online.
